about the project:

Queda Entre Nos is a podcast dedicated to the discussion of LGBTQIA+ issues and topics revolving the Latin American and intersectional experience of the community.

Our daily activities were:

  • Researching;
  • Writing and Planning of each Episode;
  • Searching for suitable Guests to elaborate on each topic;
  • Social Media Management;
  • Content Creation;
  • Identity Design.

I was also the composer of the Intro Music.

The podcast is available on

  • Anchor;
  • Spotify;
  • Apple Podcasts;
  • Google Podcasts;
  • Castbox;
  • Pocket Casts;
  • Radio Public;
  • Stitcher.

We produced and hosted more than 30 episodes.

Publishing a new episode each sunday for a year, with more than 12 guests relevant to the episode they were invited to.
