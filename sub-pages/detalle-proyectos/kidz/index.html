Luca Bermejo & KIDZ

about the project:

Art is a fundamental medium through which we conceive and express ourselves. We aim to create a collective in touch with social and aesthetic awareness. We want to de-naturalize dualistic constructs by appropriating the contents of the mass culture that holds us subject, baring it of its nature and transforming it.

We do not discriminate nor do we exclude; belonging to KIDZ is not a matter of age, sexual orientation, gender identity or social class, we seek only to gather like minded free spirits, of curious disposition and a willingness to promote acceptance.

We intend to leave a mark that portrays the intensity and vast amount of possibilities which characterize the times we live in.

We believe in love, understanding it as a free state based on equality, respect, transparency and the understanding of others.

We encourage the creation of platforms where teenage and young people can be heard, a space where each individual's personal battle can be externalized and together we can forge a better quality of life for everyone.

Internet is viewed as a tool that brings people closer, aids communication and allows us to create and express ourselves freely. But for all its advantages there are negative aspects as well. We denounce the use of technology and the web as a means of ostentation and constant comparison with others that breeds low self esteem and a false sense of self worth, as well as insecurities, therefore hindering the possibility for people to construct their identities freely. We want to turn away from nocive competition and promote an environment for creation, mobilization and love.

Our ludicrous attitude towards life is our biggest weapon. We want to shout our ideas with freedom. We act for ourselves, defending the causes we believe in, taking our ideals to the outside world to proclaim and celebrate them.
